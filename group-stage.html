Group Stage

Liverpools draw. Group of death

LFC were put into the group of death alongside PSG, Napoli and Cverna Zvezda

The Napoli games

Napoli had beaten Liverpool 1-0 in matchday 2 thanks to a 90' winner from insigne.However, Liverpool got the last laugh when salah scored the winner in a 1-0 game at Anfield to progress into the KO stage, Napoli fell to UEL

Star-Studded Paris. Klopps Toughest test

Liverpool beat PSG at Anfield 3-2 with a last minute winner from roberto firmino. in the reverse fixture in paris PSG won 2-1

Underdogs. Cverna Zvezda

Cverna was seen as an easy task for any big team but they proved everyone wrong when they beat liverpool 2-0 at Home with Pavlov scoring a brace. LFC won 4-0 at Anfield in the previous matchday
